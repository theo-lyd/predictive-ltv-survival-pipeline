# Executive Report for Non-Technical Stakeholders

## Purpose
This report explains the project in business language for executives, managers, and other non-technical stakeholders.

## One-Sentence Summary
The project builds a decision system that shows whether aggressive customer discounts create durable growth or short-lived revenue that churns quickly.

## Why the Project Exists
Subscription businesses often focus on booking new customers, but not all customers are equally valuable. A large discount may help close a deal quickly, yet it can also attract customers who leave soon after the promotion ends. This project helps leadership see the trade-off clearly and make better pricing and retention decisions.

## Business Value
- Improve LTV/CAC by prioritizing healthy cohorts.
- Reduce churn from low-quality promotional growth.
- Standardize KPI definitions across Finance, Sales, and RevOps.
- Give leadership a forward-looking view instead of only historical reporting.

## How the System Works
1. Collect raw billing, marketing, and churn data.
2. Clean and standardize the data so everyone sees the same numbers.
3. Calculate customer lifetime value, churn risk, and survival curves.
4. Present the results in an executive dashboard with AI-written summaries.
5. Monitor the pipeline so the numbers stay fresh and trustworthy.

## Decisions the Business Can Make
- Which discount levels are safe to use.
- Which marketing channels create the best long-term value.
- Which customers need retention intervention.
- How discount policy changes will affect future revenue.

## Main KPI Set
- LTV/CAC ratio.
- Net Revenue Retention.
- Churn by discount tier.
- Discount efficiency score.
- Margin by cohort.

## Expected Outcomes
- Better pricing discipline.
- Lower churn from unprofitable cohorts.
- More efficient budget allocation.
- Stronger trust in reporting and board metrics.

## Bottom Line
This project is an economic safeguard. It helps the business grow in a way that is more profitable, more predictable, and easier to manage.
